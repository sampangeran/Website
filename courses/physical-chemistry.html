Physical Chemistry (Kimia Fisik)

Kursus ini akan membahas topik mengenai Sifat Fisik dan Larutan Kimia, Teorema Kinetik Gas, Elektrokimia, serta Ilmu Antarmuka

References:

  1. Maron, S. H., Lando, J. B., & Prutton, C. F. (1974). Fundamentals of physical chemistry. New York: Macmillan.
  2. Levine, I., Physical Chemistry, Mc Graw Hill, 6th ed., 2008
  3. Bahl, B. S., Tuli, G. D., and Bahl, A., Essensial of Physical Chemistry, S Chand & Co. Ltd., 2000
  4. Smith, J. M., Van Ness, H. C., Abbott, M. M., & Swihart, M. T. (2018). Introduction to Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics (8th ed.). McGraw-Hill.
  5. Day, R. A., & Underwood, A. L. (1967). Quantitative analysis [by] R.A. Day, Jr. [and] A.L. Underwood. Englewood Cliffs, N.J: Prentice Hall.

Physical and Chemical Properties of Solutions

To learn about it, first students must be introduced to:

  1. Units of Concentrations
  2. Colligative Properties of Electrolyte and Non-electrolyte
  3. Raoult's and Henry's Law
  4. Partial Molar Properties
  5. Partition Coefficient
  6. Enthalpy Change of Solutions

Kinetics Theorem of Gases

This session will discuss materials in:

  1. Ideal Monoatomic, Diatomic, and Polyatomic Gases
  2. Pressure, Temperature, and RMS-Velocity
  3. The Equipartition of Energy Theorem
  4. Internal Energy
  5. Heat Capacity of Ideal Gases

Electrochemistry

This session will discuss materials in:

  1. Characterizing Oxidation/Reduction Reactions
  2. Electrochemical and Electrolytic Cells
  3. Thermodynamics of Electrochemistry
  4. Calculating Standard Potentials of Electrochemical Cells
  5. Nernst Law
  6. Application of Electrochemistry in Daily Life

Interfacial Sciences

This session incdludes materials in:

  1. Surface Tension and Capillarity
  2. Adsorption: Models & Mechanisms
  3. Liquid-liquid Interface
  4. Gas-solid Interface
  5. Liquid-solid Interface
  6. Colloidal Sciences
