TORCAMP Introduction to Aspen HYSYS

TORCAMP Introduction to Aspen HYSYS is a 3 day bootcamp conducted online with a purpose to introduce the Aspen HYSYS process simulator software to chemical engineers throughout Indonesia. TORCAMP is intended for beginners who want to learn the Aspen HYSYS software and need it help to do factory simulation in this process simulator.

Date

23rd – 25th February 2023

Time

19.00 – 21.00 WIB

Pricing

Rp525.000 Rp129.000

Registration

Closed


About the Speakers

Samuel Pangeran Aletheia, S.T., M.T.

Sampang is a process engineer and is currently working on the company's Jambaran-Tiung Biru Gas Unitization project PT Pertamina EP Cepu. Sampang has experienced using Aspen HYSYS for more than 5 years and received a certificate "Aspen Certified User" from Aspen University for Aspen HYSYS software.
